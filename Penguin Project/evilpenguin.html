Batman's Enemy: Penguin

Not all penguins are cute and friendly looking. Meet Penguin, a fictional character from DC Comics, commonly depicted as Batman’s enemy. Penguin is a Gotham City mobster who has a signature, ‘penguin-like’ look.

The Penguin (Oswald Chesterfield Cobblepot) character made his first appearance in Detective Comics (December 1941) and was created by Bob Kane and Bill Finger. The Penguin is one of Batman's most enduring enemies and belongs to the collective of adversaries that make up Batman's rogues gallery. Read more about Penguin the character here.
